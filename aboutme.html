About Me

Hello! My name is Doris, and I am a data analyst with a passion for using data to uncover insights and make informed decisions. I specialize in data manipulation, statistical analysis, and data visualization. I have experience working with tools such as SQL, Python, STATA, and Excel to analyze datasets and create meaningful reports.

My goal is to leverage my analytical skills to help organizations make data-driven decisions and improve their business outcomes. I enjoy solving complex problems and turning raw data into actionable insights.

When I'm not analyzing data, you can find me exploring new technologies, creating art and working with graphic design. I believe in continuous learning and am always looking for new ways to improve my skills and knowledge. Also, my focus is to combine tennis and art. I have been in tennis for more than 20 years, and for me it's such a special sport, it can be elegant but also funny. Exclusive and posh but also open to everyone. I love that anyone can play it, regardless of age. In my free time, you will find me on the tennis courts or on the slopes.
